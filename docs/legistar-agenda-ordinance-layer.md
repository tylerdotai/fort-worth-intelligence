# Legistar / Agenda / Ordinance Layer

## Core source
- Canonical source: `https://fortworthgov.legistar.com`
- Tier: A
- Type: portal / docs

## Why this layer matters

This is where city decisions become visible in a structured way.

It is the most important change-intelligence source in the Fort Worth stack.

## What to capture
- meetings
- agenda items
- minutes
- legislation references
- committee / board actions
- dates and deadlines
- departments involved
- district or geographic relevance when inferable

## Why it is high leverage

A city intelligence product becomes much more valuable when it can answer:
- what changed this week?
- what is moving through council?
- what agenda items mention this neighborhood, district, or issue?
- which department is behind a given item?

## Product implication
- municipal watchtower
- ordinance tracker
- board / commission tracker
- planning and zoning alert system
