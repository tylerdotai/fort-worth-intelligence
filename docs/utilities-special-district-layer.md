# Utilities / Water / Special District Layer

## Why this layer matters

This is where local governance gets confusing fast.

Residents and even investors often do not know:
- which utility district serves them
- whether they are inside a MUD
- which water authority affects them
- which emergency services district covers them

## Core institutions
- Tarrant Regional Water District (TRWD)
- Tarrant ESD 1
- WMUD and other MUDs surfaced by the DAO config
- city utility and service pages where relevant

## What to capture
- official site
- district/service area references
- governing board references
- service / billing / infrastructure context
- public notices and meeting materials

## Product implication
- address-to-utility resolver
- special district risk/context layer for property diligence
- infrastructure and service context for regional intelligence
