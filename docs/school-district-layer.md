# School District Layer

## Why this layer matters

School district governance is one of the most practical civic data layers for real people.

It affects:
- families
- homebuyers
- tax awareness
- local elections
- relocation decisions

## Major institutions in scope
- Fort Worth ISD
- Arlington ISD
- Mansfield ISD
- Keller ISD
- HEB ISD
- Birdville ISD
- Northwest ISD
- Crowley ISD
- Azle ISD
- Weatherford ISD
- Aledo ISD
- TCCD

## What to capture
- district official site
- board meeting pages
- agenda/minutes pages
- bond/election pages
- district metadata
- attendance / service area references where available

## Product implication
- family relocation intelligence
- district governance monitor
- school board and bond tracker
